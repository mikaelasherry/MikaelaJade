Materials & provenance

Every claim
has a receipt.

We don't say "sustainably sourced" and leave it there. Every material has a named origin, a certified supply chain, and a story we can tell in full.

Recycled 925 sterling

SCS-certified recycled sterling. Refined from recovered electronics. Chemically identical to mined silver. Audited annually.

Semi-precious gemstones

Sourced from named mines and small-scale trusted suppliers. Every stone has a provenance document. Country of origin minimum.

Made by hand

Small-batch. Certified workshop. Fair wages verified. Each piece is made to order — we don't hold excess inventory.

Packaging

Recycled kraft. Linen wrap. Seed paper intention card — plant it when you're done. Soy ink. Zero plastic.
